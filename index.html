    Our Main Goals and Method/Strategy

    AtletPosing is Denmark's leading posing brand. We assist male athletes in Men's Physique, Classic Physique, and Bodybuilding. Our main goal is to elevate the average posing level on Danish stages. Our approach to learning, correcting, and coaching posing is significantly different from the existing methods.

    At AtletPosing, we focus on your posing development through a customized process, not just "one-time corrections." We achieve this through our unique "4-Step Strategy," which ensures you reach your goal.

    4-Step Strategy

    1. Analysis of physique and current posing level.
    2. Customized posing programming schedule.
    3. Weekly posing sessions with corrections and in-depth explanations for each correction, ensuring progressive learning and understanding.
    4. Virtual analysis feedback and evaluation from our posing sessions.

    About AtletPosing

    My name is Claes Nielsen, and I am behind AtletPosing. I have studied posing for half a decade and compete in Classic and Men’s Physique myself. I’m not exactly sure where my early passion for posing came from, but I became obsessed with it, as if I was born for it. The ability to create an illusion with your own body and outshine a better athlete just by showcasing your physique fascinated me. As I grew older and noticed the lack of understanding and knowledge about posing, I knew it was my mission to bring this to Danish stages. I continuously improved my own posing daily, attended courses, and learned from the best posing coaches in the world, including Jon Lofthouse, Daniel Coffeen (from J3U), and many more.

    After years of studying and practice, I started helping Danish and a few international athletes. From there, it quickly progressed, and I have now assisted many athletes in bringing their best package to the stage.
